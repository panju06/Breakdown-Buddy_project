About Us

Breakdown Buddy, launched on February 1st, 2025, is a smart vehicle repair and service platform based in Tamil Nadu. We provide fast, dependable roadside assistance along with vehicle pick-up and drop-off services, so you can get back on the road without hassle. Whether it's a sudden breakdown, a flat tire, engine trouble, or routine service, our trusted network of mechanics and service partners is available 24/7 to help. We're here to make vehicle core casy, stress-free, and accessible anytime, anywhere across Tamil Nadu.
